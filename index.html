Who is this guy?

My name is Andreas Pribitzer. I am a self-tought Android developer and I am out there seeking for new challenges.
During my diploma project, I was thrown in at the deep end when my mission was to develop an Android application with no more than the very basic knowledge in Java. What seemed impossible at first, developed into a passion. I fell in love with the process of developing my own application and was glad that life gave me that opportunity. It also demonstrated to me an important aspect about myself: That I am a true autodidact. So I spent hours and hours developing my own applications and learning new things (what I even enjoyed!) with the goal to reach excellence. What started as a school project, ended in a passion.
